This article examines whether llms.txt files genuinely improve AI search visibility, concluding there is currently no validated evidence they reliably boost AI citation or discovery. Google explicitly states the files are unnecessary for appearing in generative AI search results, emphasizing that proven fundamentals like content quality and technical structure remain the primary ranking factors.
